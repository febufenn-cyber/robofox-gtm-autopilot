# Learning and Portfolio Policy

Beliefs may update only from immutable, source-linked outcomes. Correlated experiments sharing one family do not count as independent evidence. Generic benchmarks remain visible and are overridden only by sufficiently relevant first-party evidence with at least 30 observations.

Portfolio scores are comparative controls, not revenue forecasts. They must include delivery economics, retention, founder attention, attribution confidence, and trust. No channel receives more than the configured concentration cap.

Optimizer output is recommendation only. It cannot change spend, launch a campaign, contact a customer, or modify a live system. A material board change creates a fresh Phase 2 decision request instead of silently executing a new strategy.
