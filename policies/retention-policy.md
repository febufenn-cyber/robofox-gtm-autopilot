# Retention Policy

The public engine must not retain CONFIDENTIAL or RESTRICTED operating data.

Private-workspace defaults:

- raw imports: shortest practical period, then delete or transform
- contactability and suppression records: retain while needed to honour consent and opt-out
- recordings and transcripts: explicit purpose, consent, access control, and deletion date required
- aggregate experiment outcomes: retain for learning
- audit events: retain without secrets or full message bodies

A retention rule must identify classification, purpose, owner, deletion trigger, and allowed processors. “Keep forever” is not an acceptable default.
