# Claims Policy

Externally usable claims require an evidence record and manual review.

The following are prohibited without specific validated evidence:

- guarantees
- unsupported percentages or savings
- universal language/accent coverage
- “zero latency” or “fully compliant” statements
- replacement claims about staff
- customer names, logos, or testimonials without permission
- fabricated case studies

Every quantitative claim must state the sample, measurement period, denominator, limitations, and evidence reference. Hypotheses may be used internally only when clearly labelled `ASSUMED` or `INFERRED`.
