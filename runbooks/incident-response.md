# Incident Response Runbook

1. Activate the global kill switch and stop workers.
2. Preserve immutable audit, queue, execution, and approval records.
3. Classify the incident: credential, privacy, execution, integrity, availability, or deployment.
4. Revoke and rotate affected credentials through founder-controlled systems.
5. Quarantine affected imports and connectors.
6. Verify truth, decision, experiment, execution, revenue, learning, and queue integrity.
7. Restore only into staging first and compare schema and canonical hashes.
8. Document scope, timeline, affected records, containment, recovery, and prevention.
9. Production recovery and public/customer communication require founder approval.
