# Prophylaxis Review

For every threat or residual-risk level of three or higher, require at least one prophylaxis entry:

- `threat`: the exact failure mechanism
- `mitigation`: a bounded preventive action
- `verification`: an observable condition proving the protection was installed

Reject vague phrases such as “be careful,” “monitor performance,” or “optimize later.” Residual risk remains the critic's honest post-mitigation assessment.
